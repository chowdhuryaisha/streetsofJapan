Who we are

Welcome to Streets of Japan! Where our goal is to give you the most authentic Japanese cuisine without having to take that pesky 14 hour flight.

We make all of our food with the best quality sushi grade fish and the freshest produce to ensure that all of our customers have the best experience possible. We encourage people of all ages to come and experience for themselves true Japanese culture!
